        O.R.  71/020/17  B A E S  Vak Ctl Unit

What a life. We barely made the line, though we did it efficiently in 5343 sillaseks which is a Vak record. “Annihilate the entire ent Aev” was the clear directive. Bombard the hordes, that’s what CAx always says. He says, “B A E S, I guess we’re going to bombard the hordes today.” And he usually grins, and then I ask “Again?” And we both have a good smile, yes, a fairly decent smile. What a life!

There have been micro - disasters before, thanks to new members to the Vak, who are just getting their hands firm in Ctl, the actual method and execution. The grand purpose. The great work.

What’s to be done? Nothing. Ctl dok camp is the same as it always has been, accordingly. Recruits complete it, or die. Then they’re with the Vak, or one of the other alleged and unknown and numerous undisclosed bodies. With the Vak, they either learn to redact, vaporize, and MOVE; or they die. “Die” is an old word, and not permitted any longer in any conversation. As of now, it is still allowed in written form. Thankfully, though unfortunately. Some of us don’t take to repatterning, though we are kept on, us Salients.

Why? Well we are quick to act, and maintain the line. The overheads are quite keen of the proficiency of every unit and I agree. It keeps me a life, and not canceled.

Today, we lost another one. But, the Aev are no problem. The sector is clear, and we will be about more situations after re-activisions.

-[N]
This is an entry from a cyborg, reintegrated under the name of B A E S. It believes itself to be native organic, with ident intact. The damaged cerebrum was replaced with a piv1 cortex reactor a.p.r, and from what can be gleaned from this ‘report’ it is functioning and has subsumed into the host matter.
